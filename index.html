The science

Built to beat hard water.

Hard water leaves hair dull, rough and full of mineral buildup. Our Korean-inspired formula lifts it away and rebuilds the cuticle for real glass-hair shine.

01 / chelator

GLDA

A gentle, biodegradable agent that binds and rinses away hard-water minerals from every strand.

02 / soothe

Heartleaf

Korea's beloved Houttuynia extract calms the scalp and reduces irritation from buildup.

03 / glow

Rice Ferment

Fermented rice water adds slip and a luminous, mirror-like finish — the heart of glass hair.

04 / strength

Silk Protein

Lightweight silk amino acids smooth the cuticle and cut frizz and tangling.

05 / repair

Ceramide NP

Restores the lipid barrier, sealing in moisture and guarding against future damage.

06 / hydrate

Panthenol

Pro-vitamin B5 hydrates deeply from the inside out for soft, resilient hair.
